Developers Cup

Developer’s Cup is an amalgamation of various contests in the fields of development such as Web Development, App Development to name a few. It boasts intuitive problems which allows one to showcase their calibre and set themselves apart from the crowd.

Why Developers Cup?

Developer’s Cup boasts challenging and interesting contest problems which will allow you to sharpen your development skills as well as your presentation skills, helping you in taking a big leap in front of the competition.

Discover

Every contest will help you gain experience working under deadlines while testing the nuances in the respective field. The series of problems in each contest will be carefully articulated to help you find missing gaps in your knowledge and fill them up.

Gain

Apart from that, the submission procedure helps you build your work history on GitHub with various interesting projects built in response to problems in the contest, allowing you to showcase them to your future employers.

Learn

Since GitHub profile has nowadays become a necessary requirement when applying for a developer’s role thus having an active GitHub profile will give you the edge. What better way to keep your profile active than taking part in contests having submissions via GitHub!

Perks

  • Each contest in Developer’s Cup will have rewards such as cash rewards and prizes for top contestants to keep everyone’s determination at peak!
  • DevelopHowTo will also be providing paid internships and job opportunities to top contestants.
